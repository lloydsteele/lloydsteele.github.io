•About Me


I am a third-year dermatology resident and Academic Clinical Fellow at the Blizard Institute and Barts, working with Prof. Edel O'Toole, and a fellow at the UK DCTN, working with Prof. Hywel Williams.

My main research interests are:
1) Computational approaches applied to skin problems
2) Evidence-based medicine - based on the notion that clinical medicine is busy, and people doing research should make their findings relevant and interpretable to practicing clinicians.

I also speak Spanish and Portuguese and like running.
