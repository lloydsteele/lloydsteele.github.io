•About Me

I am a third-year Dermatology resident and Academic Clinical Fellow at the Blizard Institute and Barts, working with Prof. Edel O'Toole. I'm also a fellow at the UK DCTN working with Prof. Hywel Williams. My main interests are in computational biology, computer vision, and evidence-based medicine. I plan to do an MSc after my third year of residency followed by a year in the US and a PhD prior to conducting both academic and clinical work in Dermatology in the future.
